OUR STORY

The GRAND MINARVA hotels is a family business founded by entrepreneurs. We have a history of over 50 years of hospitality experience, where we started with a small B&B and as the business grew, we kept true to our heritage.

We have retained our agile nature. Every day is Day One. Never resting, always challenging ourselves to be better, yet staying true to who we are.

We want to bring heartfelt hospitality to the economy hotel sector. We believe the essentials of travel speak to the heart and not just the head. A hotel should never just be a room. Price should never mean you feel like you’re just another number. We believe everyone, whenever they travel, should feel more than welcomed, they should feel like they belong

Heartfelt, sincere hospitality has shaped our, past, present and future.

It’s in our DNA.

What We Do

We believe our guests should simply get heartfelt hospitality. Our hosts provide a refreshing break from the norm. They are warm, interesting and proud to welcome you into what will be your home during your stay.
